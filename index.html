Lucknow: City of Nawabs

Lucknow, the capital city of Uttar Pradesh, is famous for its rich history, culture, and architectural heritage. It is often referred to as the "City of Nawabs" due to its association with the Nawabs of Awadh who were known for their refined taste in art, music, and cuisine.

Located on the banks of the Gomti River, Lucknow boasts of magnificent monuments, exquisite gardens, and bustling bazaars. The city is a melting pot of cultures and traditions, where one can experience the grandeur of Mughal architecture and the elegance of Awadhi cuisine.
